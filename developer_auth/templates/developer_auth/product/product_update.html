Update a product

URL:

https://E-commerceAPI.com/api/products/<slug:slug>/update/

Methods:

PATCH

PUT

URL Parameters:

Slug (String, Required): The unique identifier for the product. This is usually a URL-friendly version of the product's name (e.g., "rexona-roll-on"). It is automatically generated upon creation of a product.

Headers
API-Key: {your-APIKEY}
