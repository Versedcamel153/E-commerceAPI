Response Schema: application/json

id

Integer

A unique identifier for the cart.

items

Array

Items/products in the cart.

total_quantity

String

Quantity of a product in the cart.

total_price

String

Total price of a product in the cart.

user_id

Integer

ID of the user to whom the cart belongs to.

created_at

String(date-time)

Timestamp when the cart was created.

product

(Required)

Integer

ID of the product to add to the cart.

quantity

(Required)

Integer

QUantity of the product to be added to the cart.
